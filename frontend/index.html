The Journey

From Field to Final Sale —
Three Simple Steps

AgriFresh bridges the gap between farmers, smart cold storage, and buyers — making every harvest more profitable and less wasteful.

01

Farmer Deposits Produce

Farmers bring their harvest to an AI-certified cold warehouse. Each batch is scanned, graded, and registered on the digital ledger instantly.

02

Smart Warehouse Protects

IoT sensors monitor temperature, moisture & spoilage risk 24/7. AI alerts managers before losses happen and optimizes storage conditions automatically.

03

Sell at Peak Market Price

AI monitors mandi prices and signals the perfect time to sell. Farmers earn 25–40% more by dispatching at peak demand instead of distress selling.
